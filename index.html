The Only Workshop You'll Ever Need to Learn How to Publish Papers in High-Impact Journals: Master the 4 Principles of Professional Writing

Workshop Content:

  • Time: 09:00 - 11:00

    • Functions of the text

    • Proper citation practices

  • Module 4: Practical Steps for Submitting a Paper to a Journal
    • How to find the right topic for your paper
    • Proper paper submission: how to select reviewers
    • Choosing a journal: open access or subscription
  • Time: 16:15 - 17:00
  • Module 5: Q&A
    • Questions and Answers session
