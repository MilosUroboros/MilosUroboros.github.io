About the Speaker

Miloš Stanković holds a PhD in Psychology from Ludwig Maximilian University (LMU) in Munich. He has published numerous papers in relevant journals and serves as an ad hoc reviewer for over 30 international journals. His extensive experience in academic writing has led him to develop this webinar, aimed at helping beginners successfully publish their research papers. He is working as a postdoc at University of Regensburg. He is Consultant-Editor in Musicae Scientiae Journal.
